This is a game of solitaire played with two full decks.
The goal is to have all the cards either on the ace stacks of the correct suit in the top or on a king stack in the game area, in descending order (Q, J, 10, 9...) and alternating black and red.
Kings can only be moved onto an empty card stack in the game area and aces can only be moved to an empty ace stack at the top.
All other cards can only be moved onto a card that is of one higher face value and the opposite colour within the game area, or onto an ace stack of the correct suit with a card of one lower face value (eg a three of clubs within the game area can be moved onto either a four of hearts or diamonds, but only onto a two of clubs on an ace stack).
Click on a card face down and not covered by another card to turn it over.
Once you have done all you can with the cards already face up, click on the stack in the top left to deal out again, onto every card stack that is not topped with a king. The number displayed on that stack shows how many cards are left to deal out.
Not every game can be won. In fact, most can't. Have fun!
